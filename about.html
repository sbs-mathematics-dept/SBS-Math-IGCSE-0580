Design principles
How we think about this
🎯
Cambridge-first
Every lab maps directly to Cambridge 0580 Extended syllabus topics, paper questions, and command words. Nothing is included just because it's interesting.
👨‍🏫
Teachers lead, tech supports
Labs are designed to reinforce lessons, not replace them. Use them to introduce a topic, provide a visual during class, or as structured homework exploration.
🔢
Mathematically rigorous
Every calculation is exact. Graphs are plotted correctly. Theorems show real measurements. There are no "close enough" visuals — if it's wrong, it's misleading.
⚡
Instant access
No login. No app. No download. Students open a link in any browser and start exploring. Every second of friction removed is a second more mathematics.
📱
Works everywhere
Mobile, tablet, laptop, classroom projector. The circle theorems lab even has full touch support for dragging points on a phone screen.
🆓
Completely free
No subscriptions, no ads, no paywalls. Built and hosted on GitHub Pages — freely available to every SBS student forever.
What's included
The 8 interactive labs
🎲 Probability Lab — Simulation, tree diagrams (with & without replacement), Venn diagrams, conditional probability
📊 Graph Explorer — Plot any function, multiple curves, zero-finding, intercepts, x-value probe
📐 Transformation Lab — Translation, reflection, rotation (any centre), enlargement (including negative k)
📏 Circle Theorems Explorer — 7 theorems, fully draggable, real-time angle measurement
📈 Statistics Lab — Averages & spread, histograms with frequency density, cumulative frequency curves, box plots
📉 Functions Explorer — fg(x), gf(x), f⁻¹(x), domain & range, function transformations
🔺 Trigonometry Unit Circle — Animated 0°–360°, sin/cos/tan wave drawing, exact values at key angles
📦 3D Geometry Explorer — Volume & surface area for 6 shapes, substituted formulae, nets
