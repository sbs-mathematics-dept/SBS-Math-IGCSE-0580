The Department
People Behind the Maths
☕
Mr. Robert
Lower Secondary Mathematics
The department's most enthusiastic guide to the fundamentals. Mr. Robert specialises in Lower Secondary maths and has a remarkable gift for making Year 7 and 8 students feel like they already know what they're doing — even when they absolutely do not. Armed with patience, positivity, and a coffee mug that says "math is awesome."
💬 "Negative numbers aren't scary. They're just positive numbers having a bad day."
📐
Mr. Rene
Lower Secondary & Cambridge IGCSE Mathematics
The bridge between "I kind of get maths" and "I actually get maths." Mr. Rene teaches across Lower Secondary and IGCSE, expertly guiding students from basic algebra all the way to Cambridge exam technique. Famous for his perfectly drawn freehand circles and the dramatic pause before revealing the value of x.
💬 "x is not lost. x is just on a journey of self-discovery."
📊
Ms. Hanna
Cambridge IGCSE & A Level Mathematics
Ms. Hanna teaches at the highest levels — Cambridge IGCSE Extended and A Level — which means her students are the ones who said "I want the hard version." She delivers. Whether it's integration, vectors, or probability distributions, Ms. Hanna makes Advanced Maths feel less like a mountain and more like a very steep set of stairs. Still steep. But stairs.
💬 "The syllabus says 'prove.' I say 'let's have a conversation with the examiner.'"
🏆
Ms. Cheng
Head of Mathematics · IGCSE & A Level
Head of Department, Cambridge IGCSE 0580 and A Level specialist, and the person who built this entire website. Ms. Cheng coordinates the whole Mathematics Department while somehow also teaching IGCSE Extended and A Level — and finding time to create 9 interactive labs and 15+ lesson pages. Students describe her as: "she makes hard things feel obvious — then we look at our notes and realise how hard they actually were."
💬 "I didn't build 15 interactive lessons for you to use the textbook. Open. The. Website."
